What is online heat treatment software?

Our system software enables the professional to manage and monitor the temperature during the heat-treatment process without the need for human contact in the physical process.

Importance of online heat treatment software for your business

  • Spend a little over a conventional plotte
  • Any number of heat cycles/ curvers can be recorded
  • View live data of your HT process on your PC/Mobile
  • Cycle time can be pre-set for each individual item
  • Its that easy Choose item name on your mobile and start cycle
  • Monitors Energy for every cycle (* A network compatible energy meter required)
  • Send HT cycle reports to your customer for any of the batches
  • Save energy by monitoring and comparing each cycle
  • Option to store data on cloud if hardware not available at premises
  • Additional reports can be added and customized to specific need at a cost
  • E-mail of individual HT cycles is also possible

Who should install online Heat Treatment Monitor?

  • A factory needing to digitize and move towards upgrading and Industry 4.0
  • Better control on the process.
  • Improve Energy utilization.
  • Ease of maintaining logs and reports.
